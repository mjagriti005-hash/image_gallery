Tip No. Title Description
1 Use Natural Light Morning and evening light gives soft and beautiful colors.
2 Keep Your Camera Steady Hold your phone with both hands or use support to avoid blur.
3 Choose a Clean Background A simple background makes your subject stand out.
4 Follow the Rule of Thirds Turn on grid lines and place your subject slightly off-center.
5 Tap to Focus Tap on the screen to focus properly before capturing.
6 Take Multiple Shots Capture several angles and select the best one later.
7 Try Different Angles Experiment with high, low, and side angles for creative shots.
8 Avoid Digital Zoom Move closer instead of zooming to keep your photo sharp.
9 Clean Your Lens A clean lens improves picture clarity instantly.
10 Edit Lightly Use soft editing — avoid heavy filters.
Stay patient — nature takes time to show its magic!
